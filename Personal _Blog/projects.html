Pneumonia Detection Using X-Ray Images

X-ray images are utilized for pneumonia detection. The images are analyzed by medical professionals or machine learning algorithms, which identify specific patterns associated with pneumonia, such as consolidation or infiltrates in the lung. This aids in the accurate diagnosis and treatment of pneumonia, leading to better patient outcomes.
